Navigation Behavior
Interaction Patterns
Collapsible Main Menu: The primary navigation can be collapsed to a narrow icon bar to maximize workspace.
Context-Sensitive Secondary Menu: The law firm menu appears only when a firm is selected.
Expandable Sections: Menu items with children can be expanded/collapsed to show/hide deeper navigation options.
Tooltips: When the main menu is collapsed, tooltips appear on hover to identify icons.
Visual Indicators: Active and expanded items receive visual highlighting.
Badges: Notification counts appear as badges on relevant menu items.
